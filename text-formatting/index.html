Jane Yellowrock Review

Summary

The Jane Yellowrock series by Faith Hunter is about a Cherokee Indian born during the trail of tears who now can change into the form of a Mountain Lion. The mountain lion, beast, was magically bonded to Jane, who lived for decades in the wild before returning to her human form.

Now Jane is human, most of the time, and makes her living slaying vampires. Ironically, she works for a master vampire as a security consultant. Jane works with both humans and supernatural beings

Book 1 - Skinwalker

In Skinwalker we get to meet the major players in the series. Jane, her assistant Eli, Leo - a vampire and Master of the City of New Orleans. This book provides background and sets the tone for the series.

This quote by Jane sums up her character: Chicken, yeah, that's me. I'd rather fight an old rogue-vamp in my underwear, with my bare hands, than deal with relationship problems.
